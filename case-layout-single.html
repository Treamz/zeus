More about
NunoErin - is an award-winning design company. They change the furniture experience through its playful and interactive products. We work together on Ucreate platform and games that will change the world. The Ucreate interactive Wall Panels & Table Tops is a hybrid play and decor solution that enriches the family experience through touch based games and creative play. When not in use, the ply surface displays greetings, seasonal images, and animations, and is surrounded by whimsical graphics that are featured within the NunoErin games. We made excited and dynamic platform by which your child is immersed in the colorful world with funny animals, animated puzzles, coloring jungle and much more interesting things.
