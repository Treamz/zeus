Who we are

Zeus group is not just a group of developers, but something more incredible. We have combined the Ukrainian roots with the California spirit, which allows us to open new horizons for solving problems in software development. We strive to become better and improve the need for programming so that our clients can enjoy professional solutions. We are trusted by many global brands and we always bring projects to perfection.
The company has existed since 2008 and has already managed to recommend itself as a reliable partner for such companies as Roadcube, Amazon webservices, Investis, Android, Apple, T-Mobile, IBM. We also developed an AI application that became a finalist at IBM's Glovators challenge in 2016. We are actively involved in the development of Startups and help to look at the world with other eyes.
